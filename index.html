Welcome to Envision.

Envision is a website committed to protecting the rights of women, non gender conforming people, people of LGBTQ+ communities, and people of color. We aim to spread awareness of the issues that non-male individuals face while simultaneously providing these communities with a supportive network and abundant resources. We hope to start conversations by sharing the perspective of women and keep the conversation going by sharing up-to-date resources.
